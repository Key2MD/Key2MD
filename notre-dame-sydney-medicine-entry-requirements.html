Notre Dame Sydney

Notre Dame Sydney is one of the three postgraduate CASPer pathways.

UNDS ranks interview candidates using GPA, unweighted GAMSAT, CASPer and bonus points in a 30:30:30:10 structure.

MMI lens

Notre Dame Sydney MMI talking points: ethics, clinician-led PBL and clinical-school breadth.

Notre Dame Sydney combines small-group clinician-led PBL with clinical schools across Sydney, Melbourne and rural locations. In MMI, your answer should sound values-based and clinically curious, not just CASPer-aware.

Darlinghurst medical precinctNotre Dame describes the Darlinghurst campus as part of a prestigious medical and research precinct.
Clinician-led PBLFoundation years are delivered principally through problem-based learning in small groups led by a clinician.
Sydney, Melbourne and rural clinical schoolsClinical years draw on seven clinical schools, including St Vincent's and Mater, Auburn, Hawkesbury, Werribee Mercy, Lithgow, Ballarat and Wagga Wagga.
Ethics and careThe School emphasises individual support, caring and ethical doctors, bioethics, rural medicine, palliative care, First Nations health and applied medical research.
Interview angle: For Notre Dame Sydney, explain why ethics, patient dignity and clinical-school variety matter to how you will learn, especially if you are also relying on CASPer strength.

What Students Miss

UNDS is not just a low-combo safety school. A weak CASPer can damage an otherwise reasonable application, while a strong CASPer can keep an applicant alive at a lower combo than many GPA/GAMSAT schools.
