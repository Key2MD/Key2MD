Interview Ranking Formula

Notre Dame Sydney ranks candidates for interview using four standardised components: unweighted GAMSAT, GPA, CASPer and bonus points. This is why a Q4 CASPer can matter so much here.

ComponentWeight
Unweighted average GAMSAT30%

Bonus points10%

What Students Miss

UNDS is not just a low-combo safety school. A weak CASPer can damage an otherwise reasonable application, while a strong CASPer can keep an applicant alive at a lower combo than many GPA/GAMSAT schools.
