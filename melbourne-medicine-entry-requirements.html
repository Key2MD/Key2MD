University of Melbourne

Melbourne MD uses unweighted GAMSAT and a 50% interview offer formula.

For standard GEMSAS applicants, Melbourne ranks interview offers using GPA and equally weighted GAMSAT sections. Final offers put heavy weight on the MMI.

MMI lens

Melbourne MMI talking points: leadership, choice and clinical-research depth.

Melbourne presents its MD as a flexible, choice-rich program designed to produce well-rounded doctors who can thrive in complex environments and lead change. In MMI terms, make the case for intellectual curiosity plus grounded clinical maturity.

Flexibility and choiceThe redesigned Melbourne MD highlights choice, customisation and Discovery subjects that let students explore areas of interest.
Hospitals and research institutesMelbourne points to partnerships with leading hospitals and research institutes, with clinical schools based in affiliated teaching hospitals.
Leadership and changeThe course explicitly aims to nurture leadership abilities and prepare graduates to make a positive impact in healthcare.
Capstone readinessThe structure includes integrated bioscience and clinical learning, two core clinical years, optional research depth and a capstone semester rehearsing safe clinical practice.
Interview angle: For Melbourne, avoid a generic prestige answer. Speak to how you would use clinical-school breadth, research access and curriculum choice to become useful in complex health systems.

Special Pathways and Reranking

Graduate Access Melbourne can rerank eligible applicants based on sustained disadvantage. Melbourne also has rural access arrangements, an MD Rural Pathway, guaranteed entry pathways, and priority access for eligible Indigenous applicants.

A small number of applicants with high-level performance in a recent cognate PhD may also be reranked in their favour under strict quota limits.

What Students Miss

Melbourne is not using the ACER weighted overall GAMSAT in the ordinary way. If your section scores are uneven, your Melbourne competitiveness can differ from your headline GAMSAT.
