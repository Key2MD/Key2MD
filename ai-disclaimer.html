AI Feedback Disclaimer

Key2MD's AI tools are built to help students practise more intelligently. They are not official exam scores or admissions decisions.

Last updated: 8 May 2026.

What The AI Is For

The AI feedback tools are designed to help students notice patterns in structure, empathy, communication, reasoning, reflection and specificity. They are best used as repeated practice feedback, not as a definitive prediction of real examiner scoring.

What The AI Is Not

AI feedback is not an official CASPer score, MMI examiner mark, GAMSAT Section 2 mark, university assessment, professional assessment, medical advice, legal advice or guarantee of selection.

Why Scores May Differ From Real Exams

Real exams use confidential rubrics, live or platform-specific scoring, cohort standardisation, assessor judgement and changing institutional priorities. AI feedback cannot perfectly replicate those conditions.

How To Use It Well

Use the feedback to identify recurring weaknesses, rewrite or re-record answers, compare your trend over time, and decide when to seek human feedback. The most useful signal is repeated pattern recognition, not one isolated score.

Privacy Reminder

Avoid submitting highly sensitive personal information about yourself or others unless it is necessary for the practice task. For more detail, read the Privacy Policy.

Practise with the disclaimer in mind

Use AI as a high-volume practice partner, then use human feedback when the stakes are high or the weakness is subtle.
