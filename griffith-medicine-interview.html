How Griffith selects for medicine

Griffith offers a graduate-entry Doctor of Medicine based on the Gold Coast. Selection combines your GPA, the GAMSAT, and a multiple mini interview (MMI) for shortlisted applicants.

Official source: Griffith Medicine admissions at griffith.edu.au.

What the Griffith MMI tends to reward

In our coaching experience, Griffith's MMI rewards clear, caring, well-reasoned answers:

  • Clear reasoning - you name the tension and weigh more than one side before acting.
  • Professionalism - sensible handling of safety, confidentiality and competing interests.
  • Human communication - plain, warm explanation over jargon.
  • Genuine engagement with the person in role-play stations.

Common mistakes we see

  • Reaching for jargon instead of clear explanation.
  • Generic answers that could fit any applicant.
  • Not engaging with the person in front of you.
  • Answers that sound rehearsed and break under a follow-up.

About the Griffith MD program

Griffith's Doctor of Medicine is a graduate-entry program of around four years, based on the Gold Coast with placements across South-East Queensland including regional sites. Teaching is integrated and case-based with early clinical exposure. Confirm the current structure with Griffith.

Fees (approximate)

Most domestic students study in a Commonwealth Supported Place (CSP) and pay the government-set annual student contribution for medicine - roughly AUD 10,000-11,000 per year as a 2025 guide, subject to change. Full-fee domestic and international places cost substantially more. For orientation only; confirm current fees with Griffith.

Research opportunities

Griffith offers research and honours-style options alongside the MD, with links to its health institutes. Confirm current options with Griffith.

What students say (general perception)

The points below summarise common themes in public student discussion - opinions, not facts, and your experience may differ. Form your own view via open days and current students.

  • Commonly praised: a pleasant Gold Coast location, modern facilities and a supportive cohort.
  • Commonly raised as challenges: placement travel across South-East Queensland for some students.
