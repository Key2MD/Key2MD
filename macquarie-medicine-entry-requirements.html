Macquarie University

Macquarie Medicine uses weighted GPA, GAMSAT and capped GPA bonuses.

Macquarie's MD is full-fee and has a small set of published GPA bonus cohorts, capped at 5% total.

Macquarie GPA Bonuses

Macquarie applies a 3% bonus to weighted GPA for each listed cohort, capped at 5% total. The bonus is not available to international applicants.

CohortBonus
Aboriginal and/or Torres Strait Islander applicant3% to weighted GPA
Rural applicant3% to weighted GPA
Macquarie Bachelor of Clinical Science applicant3% to weighted GPA
More than one cohortCapped at 5% total

What Students Miss

Macquarie also reserves some interview places for Macquarie Bachelor of Clinical Science graduates or final-year students. That is not the same thing as a universal score multiplier, so it should be read as a pathway advantage rather than a clean combo conversion.
