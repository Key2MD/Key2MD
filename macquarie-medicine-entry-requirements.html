Macquarie University

Macquarie Medicine uses weighted GPA, GAMSAT and capped GPA bonuses.

Macquarie's MD is full-fee and has a small set of published GPA bonus cohorts, capped at 5% total.

Check Macquarie in the calculatorOfficial GEMSAS source
MMI lens

Macquarie MMI talking points: global exposure, digital health and patient safety.

Macquarie positions its MD as global, future-focused and embedded in a university-led academic health sciences centre. For MMI, the strongest answers link cultural responsiveness, health systems thinking and patient safety.

India and global health systemsMacquarie describes structured Australian and international clinical experiences, including Apollo Hospital in Hyderabad, India.
Sydney clinical ecosystemAustralian clinical learning environments include Macquarie University Health, Northern Sydney Local Health District, Northern Beaches Hospital and MindSpot Clinic.
Modern facilities and technologyMacquarie promotes simulation spaces, robotic surgical systems and gamma knife access as part of its technology-rich training environment.
Research-active patient careMacquarie University Health brings together clinical care, teaching and research in a university-led academic health sciences centre.
Interview angle: For Macquarie, use the MMI to show curiosity about different health systems and cultures, then bring it back to safer patient-centred care in multicultural Australia.

Macquarie GPA Bonuses

Macquarie applies a 3% bonus to weighted GPA for each listed cohort, capped at 5% total. The bonus is not available to international applicants.

CohortBonus
Aboriginal and/or Torres Strait Islander applicant3% to weighted GPA
Rural applicant3% to weighted GPA
Macquarie Bachelor of Clinical Science applicant3% to weighted GPA
More than one cohortCapped at 5% total

What Students Miss

Macquarie also reserves some interview places for Macquarie Bachelor of Clinical Science graduates or final-year students. That is not the same thing as a universal score multiplier, so it should be read as a pathway advantage rather than a clean combo conversion.
