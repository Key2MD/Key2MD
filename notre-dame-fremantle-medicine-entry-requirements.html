Notre Dame Fremantle

Notre Dame Fremantle combines GPA, GAMSAT, CASPer and bonus points.

UNDF uses the same broad 30:30:30:10 interview-ranking structure as Sydney, with Fremantle-specific bonus considerations including WA residency.

Quick Read

Minimum GPAWeighted GPA of at least 5.2.
GAMSATUnweighted average, total at least 52 and minimum 50 in each subsection.
CASPerExplicit 30% component in interview ranking.
Bonus pointsRurality, WA residency and completed HDR can matter.

ComponentWeight

Fremantle-Specific Bonus Flags

Fremantle applicants should check rurality, Western Australian residency, completed higher degrees by research, and any relevant UNDA facilitated interview pathway. KCRMT/Broome preferences have additional considerations and should be checked directly.

What Students Miss

UNDF can be a real opportunity for applicants with strong CASPer and relevant bonus points. But a low CASPer can hurt badly because it is not a minor side criterion.
