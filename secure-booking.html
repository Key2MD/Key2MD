First session - secured booking

Let's lock in your first session properly.

First-time bookings are secured with a $300 deposit. It goes straight onto your session - it is not an extra charge - and it lets me hold the time for you with confidence.

First, thank you for choosing to work with me - I genuinely look forward to it. I just need to be upfront about one thing before we book.

I am a current medical student, and through peak MMI and interview season I have very little free time. When a session is booked, I plan my day, my study and my other students around it. When a first-time booking is cancelled at the last minute or quietly forgotten, that hour cannot be refilled - it is genuinely lost time and lost income at the busiest point of my year. I have had this happen enough times that I can no longer absorb it, and I am sorry to have to put a guard in place.

So that I can keep saying yes to students and protect everyone's time, first-time sessions are now secured with a $300 deposit. It is fully applied to your session - you are not paying anything extra - it simply confirms the booking is real on both sides. I hope that feels fair; it is the only way I can keep offering 1:1 time reliably right now.
