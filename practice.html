RACGP Doctor-calibrated feedback

Unlock unlimited RACGP-mode reviews calibrated for AGPT/FSP doctors: empathy, rationale, uncertainty, dignity and human tone.

This isn't generic AI feedback. Dan has spent 300+ hours developing the marking criteria, prompt architecture, and scoring rubrics that power this tool - drawing directly from his experience coaching 200+ students through CASPer and MMI.

Every response is assessed using Dan's Q1-Q4 practice framework, the same teaching lens he uses in live 1:1 sessions. The AI is specifically trained to penalise the exact mistakes Dan sees students make - robotic framework-speak, buzzword answers, lack of genuine empathy, and spending all your time on one prompt while neglecting the other - things ChatGPT would happily praise.

Both prompts matter. CASPer stations usually test whether you can divide attention under time pressure. The marker now treats prompt 1 and prompt 2 as equal-value parts of the station, so a polished answer to only one question cannot carry the whole mark. Think of it like diminishing returns on investment: once one answer is strong enough, extra time there gives less benefit than moving on and earning the marks available in the second question.

Dan is generally sceptical of AI feedback. Most AI marking tools give vague, encouraging responses that don't actually help you improve. This one was built with the opposite philosophy: honest, specific, sometimes uncomfortable feedback - because that's what moves scores.

Try it yourself - paste the same response into ChatGPT and compare. You'll see the difference immediately.

New to MMI practice?

A quick 60-second orientation

An MMI is a circuit of short stations. At each one you read a scenario, then talk through your answer out loud while we record it. Here is how a station runs:

1
Read the scenario during a short reading window. Jot notes in the scratchpad.
2
Answer out loud to the camera as you would to an examiner. The timer keeps you to interview pace.
3
Get feedback on your content and your delivery, with a stronger rewrite of what you actually said.

Not sure of your university's exact format? Start with our standard setup. You can switch to a custom timer any time.
