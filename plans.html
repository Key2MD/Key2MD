It is not only what you say

The MMI is a spoken, face-to-face station, so examiners mark a single holistic impression - and how you deliver an answer is a large part of it.

Your tone, your prosody (the rise, fall and stress in your voice), your rhythm and pace, and the presence and composure you carry decide whether the same words land as warm and convincing or flat and rehearsed. Strong content delivered poorly rarely scores well. Premium reads these delivery signals directly; every tier sharpens the structure and substance they sit on.

Your first review is free

Record a station response and submit it - your first AI-marked Transcript review is free, no credit needed. One per account, no expiry.

After that, MMI feedback uses credits or an MMI Pro subscription. Spoken-response marking includes real-time transcription and per-prompt criterion analysis, so it costs more to run than a CASPer text review.

Why does Premium cost more?

Transcript feedback analyses what you said. Premium goes further - it simultaneously processes your voice patterns, pacing, and delivery alongside a visual read of your presence and composure. Running all of those analyses in parallel requires significantly more compute than text alone. The price reflects what it actually costs to run, not a margin decision.
