Why does Premium cost more?

Transcript feedback analyses what you said. Premium goes further - it simultaneously processes your voice patterns, pacing, and delivery alongside a visual read of your presence and composure. Running all of those analyses in parallel requires significantly more compute than text alone. The price reflects what it actually costs to run, not a margin decision.

What the full mock report includes
The debrief no coach gives you after a mock - because it can't be done manually.
  • *
    Criterion heatmap - your average Empathy, Reasoning, Reflection scores across every station. Instantly see where you're consistent vs where you drop off.
  • *
    Stamina pattern - did your quality hold across the full exam or decline after station 4? Most students don't know this about themselves until they're sitting the real one.
  • *
    Cross-station patterns - recurring habits that show up across multiple stations. "Your reflection was strong on Stations 2 and 5 but missing on 1, 3, and 4 - this is a trigger-specific gap, not a knowledge gap."
  • *
    Category weaknesses - which station types cost you marks. Role-play? Policy? Motivation? You'll know, and you'll know what to fix.
  • *
    Key2MD cohort interpretation - your overall mark is interpreted against a competitive cohort of real applicants actively preparing. 6.5+ is treated as a likely Q4-style signal if repeatable; 5.5-6.5 is probably Q3-style. Not an official Acuity score.
  • *
    The one thing to fix before your real interview - a single, prioritised action. Not a list of ten. The one thing that would move your score most if you fixed it today.
