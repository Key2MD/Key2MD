Why this isn't ChatGPT
Built to give you uncomfortable feedback. On purpose.

Most AI feedback tools give vague, encouraging responses that don't actually help. This one was built with the opposite philosophy.

Dan's Q1–Q4 Scoring Framework
Q4
Excellent — strong judgment, genuine empathy, rarely misses key considerations.
Q3
Good — sound reasoning, adequate empathy. May miss nuance in one area.
Q2
Developing — some structure but surface-level. Misses important stakeholders.
Q1
Needs work — missing core elements, little empathy, or misreads the scenario.
Empathy is weighted more heavily than standard rubrics. Answers that are technically sound but emotionally cold will not reach Q4 — because that's what real markers do.
