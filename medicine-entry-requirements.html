Start With The Formula, Then Check The Exceptions

Most graduate-entry medical schools use some version of a GPA plus GAMSAT ranking before interview. The trap is that each school handles the details differently: weighted versus unweighted GAMSAT, GPA weighting, CASPer, rural pathways, higher-degree rules, and bonus points.

The pages below turn those rules into a practical applicant view, then link into the Key2MD calculator so you can test your own numbers.

Use official sources before applying. These pages are plain-English summaries of public admissions information. Always check the relevant GEMSAS and university pages before submitting preferences or evidence.
