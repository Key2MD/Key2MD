University of Queensland

UQ Medicine uses unweighted GAMSAT and key-degree GPA.

UQ is especially important for Honours and higher-degree applicants because some key degrees are deemed to specific GPA values.

MMI lens

UQ MMI talking points: early patient contact, learning communities and Queensland scale.

UQ describes a clinically contextualised MD with early patient contact, research, electives and learning communities. For MMI, the best frame is how you would stay patient-centred inside a large, high-opportunity clinical system.

Early patient contactUQ highlights integrated, clinically contextualised learning with early patient contact.
Learning communityStudents join a learning community described as a clinical home for ongoing learning through the program.
Rural Clinical School scaleUQ Rural Clinical School is described as the largest rural clinical school in Australia hosted by a single university, with sites including Bundaberg, Hervey Bay, Rockhampton and Toowoomba.
Global and Ochsner optionsUQ describes Ochsner clinical school opportunities in New Orleans, including the UQ-Ochsner pathway and Year 4 placement options subject to availability.
Interview angle: For UQ, connect scale with humility: teamwork, early patient contact, rural exposure, research and the discipline to keep patients at the centre of complex systems.

UQ GPA Overrides

UQ can treat specific completed key degrees as a deemed GPA. This is why a first-class Honours result can be strategically huge for UQ applicants.

Qualification as key degreeUQ deemed GPA
Honours Class I7.00
Honours Class IIA6.00
Honours Class IIB5.00
Honours Class III4.00
AQF Level 10 research masters or doctoral degree7.00

What Students Miss

The ACER overall score is not the score UQ uses. If Section 3 is carrying your weighted score, your UQ result can be weaker than expected. If S1 and S2 are strong, UQ may look better than your overall GAMSAT suggests.
